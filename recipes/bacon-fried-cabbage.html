Bacon-Fried Cabbage

Description

Cabbage and bacon make a comforting winter side dish that goes well with any meat dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices thick-cut bacon, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 small cabbage, cored and sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Cook bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until fat starts to render, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in cabbage, onion, caraway seeds, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook, covered, stirring occasionally, until cabbage is tender, about 15 minutes.
